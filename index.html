The Challenge & Our Solution

Precision Detection for Sustainable Agriculture

An excessive and unnecessary amount of preventative pesticides are currently used in the agricultural industry. Associated with this are negative environmental and health consequences, alongside financial losses.

sanSpore aims to develop a product that incorporates machine learning and sensor technology to precisely detect harmful spore presence. Early detection enables:

  • Smarter, targeted use of pesticides
  • Optimal humidity control, reducing energy consumption
  • Insightful, actionable data on plant health
